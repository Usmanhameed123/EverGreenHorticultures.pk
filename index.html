Who we are

Convert your home into a green modern house!

Evergreen Horticultures is Pakistan’s one of biggest nursery farm and horticulture supplier founded in 1995 with many successful projects in private and government sector. Evergreen Horticultures mainly prepare and grow plants for our government and private clients in our nursery farms located in Pattoki, Lahore and Islambad We have provided our Landscape or horticulture services to many residential projects, private and government housing societies, and government plantation projects all over country At Evergreen Horticultures we provide services in the field of enhanced commercial and residential landscaping across Pakistan; in short, our brilliant team of professionals makes your property more valuable, lavish and aesthetic by creating inventive and customized masterpieces for your residential or commercial land. We offer a plethora of landscaping services and solutions to our esteemed customers. Standing with our clients at all stages of any landscaping and horticulture related project, be its development, implementation or management, we at Evergreen Horticultures provide means for your dream landscape to turn into reality in the most structured and feasible of ways with the help of our qualified team of more than 10 personnel and a labour force of almost 500 workers. Instead of making every corner look the same, we strive to work on the comprehensive look your landscape emits.
